Snacks.io

"The Best Snacks" project

I am a snacks lover, and sometimes will skip meals for snacking. I love chookie, biscotti, nougat, chocolate, nuts, green tea cake, mango smoothie, yogurt, ice cream... yes, anything that is sweet. I‘m also always terribly indecisive when doing snacks shopping, especially when I first came to US (the only American snack I knew then was OREO cookie). I could spend hours trying to look at every single snack on every shelf at safeway before I exhaustedly go to checkout. And the worst thing is: the snacks I end up buying taste nasty! After several horrible snacking experience, I decided to resolve my snacking crisis with friends who are also big snacks lovers.

Snacks.io is a simple app to share your snacking experience with other snack lovers. Next time when you are hesitate about what snacks to buy, get inspiration from your snack.io community. Once you have tried a new snack, take a picture and rate it good or bad. Know what snacks to avoid and never miss a must-try. Let's always enojoy an exceptional snacking experience.

Observation and Interview

We started our observation by walking into supermarkets and snack stores as general guests, and spent hours there observing customers. I also randomly invited some customers and my friends for interview and chose "your last snacking experience" as my starting point to dig deeper into their needs.
