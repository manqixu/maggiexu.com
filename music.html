Face the Music

Art of Design for Interactive Computer Music

This quarter at Stanford, I am taking MUSIC256A(CS476A): Art of Design for Computer Music. It's never fun to code in C++ but I'm enjoying the exploration of interesting, sometimes weird ways to interact with music. So far I've been using JUCE, OpenFrameworks, Javascript to prototype those ideas.

I played violin for 7 years. This is my first time to learn computer-generated music. For my final project, I would like to build a music app where you use your face to create a song, together with other users who might be in the other side of the world. Let see how it goes!
